Verdana

ABOUT

Verdana is a humanist san-serif typeface designed for Microsoft Corporation by Matthew
Carter in 1996. It was designed to be readable at a small size at a low resolution screen.
The typeface brought the attention of Virginia Howlett, Microsoft’s typography group.
The name “Verdana” is based on verdant (something green), and “Ana” the name of
Howlett’s oldest daughter.
